Edith Okubanjo World Ministry

Rev Edith Adefunke Okubanjo received the call of God upon her life in 1982 while she was studying abroad in the United States of America.

On that fateful night, our Lord Jesus Christ appeared to her as she slept, anointed her with oil, and instructed her to go and preach the gospel; to heal the sick, and set those who have been held spiritually captive free. She accepted her charge, and has been doing so ever since.

Through her unceasing ministrations and efforts many sick have been healed in the name of Jesus Christ of Nazareth.
